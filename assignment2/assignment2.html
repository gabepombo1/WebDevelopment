Description:

This game is about a sane Harvester robot named Jank! All his robot buddies have gone Haywire! Including his big boss, Crank. All he can do now is defend himself. Perhaps if he builds himself up, he could eventually face the Mother AI responsible for all of this, and get to the bottom of it.

Instructions:

The game is played with the WASD keys.

"W" moves the bot forwards, "S" moves the bot backwards, "D" moves the bot to the right, "A" moves the bot to the left.

Note:

The camera does not move. Turning off Adblock might help with the game not playing.
